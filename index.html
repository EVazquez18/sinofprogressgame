About The Game

Play as a young necromancer with the power to pick of the pieces of a shattered humanity. Choose your own fate; choose your own story.

Sin of Progress is a 2-D text-based adventure game based on the story written by Esme Vazquez. It is the story of a young woman, named Renlin Rankin, who is an archetype, a being born of the death of humanity. The archetypes represent the pieces of humanity that have been lost, each an individual with a symbolic existance and a extrordinary power. It's up to you to reunite them to save what's left of the human race - are you up for the challenge?

Immersive Story

Play an immersive storyline that allows you to choose the path of the protagonist. Make challenging decisions, find rewarding conclusions.

Engaging Combat

Fight a terrifying cast of enemies; teach those who mean you harm of your incredible power.

Captivating Art

Engage in the captivating art style of this illustrated story book; let it take your mind to worlds unexplored.
